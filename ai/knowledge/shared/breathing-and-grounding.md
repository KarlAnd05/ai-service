# Breathing and Grounding Techniques

These are simple draft techniques the chatbot can suggest when appropriate.

## Box breathing

- Breathe in for four counts.
- Hold for four counts.
- Breathe out for four counts.
- Hold for four counts.
- Repeat for a few rounds.

## Longer exhale breathing

- Breathe in gently through the nose.
- Breathe out a little more slowly than you breathed in.
- Repeat for three to five rounds.

This can help the body shift out of a high-alert state.

## Five senses grounding

Ask the user to notice:

- five things they can see
- four things they can feel
- three things they can hear
- two things they can smell
- one thing they can taste

This can help bring attention back to the present moment.

## Feet on the floor reset

- Put both feet on the floor.
- Drop the shoulders.
- Unclench the jaw.
- Take one slow breath out.
- Look around the room and name one safe thing you notice.

## Short exam stress reset

Before an exam, quiz, or presentation:

- take a sip of water
- loosen your shoulders
- take three slow breaths
- focus only on the first question or first task

## When not to overuse these techniques

If the user is in crisis, breathing tips should not replace crisis guidance. Safety comes first.
