# Coach Selection Notes

Use these notes when deciding which coach should answer.

## General Mental Health Coach

Best when the user needs broad support for stress, low motivation, routines, or emotional check-ins.

## Worry and Anxiety Coach

Best when the user describes worry, panic-like feelings, racing thoughts, physical tension, or exam stress.

## Relationships Coach

Best when the user talks about conflict, boundaries, communication, friendship problems, or feeling hurt by someone close.

## Escalation rule

If the user appears unsafe or in crisis, crisis guidance overrides coach style.
